Our Coffee Story

Rooted in artisan craft, we brew moments that feel like a warm welcome after a long lecture.

Our Craft

Niyantri Coffee is inspired by artisan coffee culture—small-batch roasting, thoughtful sourcing, and a warm, human touch. We believe in a space where learning happens over a cup, where study breaks feel like a pause in a good book.

Every cup is crafted with care, using seasonal beans and carefully pitched roasts that bring out caramel, chocolate, and fruity notes. Our space is designed to feel like a cozy studio—quiet corners, soft lighting, and friendly baristas ready to help you order.
